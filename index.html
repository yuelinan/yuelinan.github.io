Biography

I am an associate professor at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Southeast University. I am also a member of PALM group working with Prof Min-Ling Zhang. Before that, I received my PhD degree from University of Science and Technology of China in December, 2024, advised by Prof Qi Liu. I received my Bachelor degree from HoHai University in July, 2019, and majored in Computer Science and minored in Law.

My research interests include Natural Language Processing, Trustworthy AI and AI for Social Science.

We are looking for self-motivated students! If you are interested, please feel free to contact me!
